Polystarch Portion Bread

Description

Polystarch Portion Bread: While it may look like simple fare, and it is, this portion bread is highly sought after on it's home planet Jakku. Once popular among the New Republic and Galactic Empire troops as a quick battle field meal, this commodity has come to rule much of the trade of Jakku due to sparse supplies of much else. A hearty instant bread loaf that requires only two ingredients and one step to complete, this will satiate the hunger of any weary traveler or scavenger.

Ingredients

  • Polystarch flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add polystarch flour to small serving of water and mix together. Watch the chemical reaction work it's magic.
  2. Enjoy.
